The Ultra-High
Temperature
Materials Database

Unlocking the potential of extreme materials

The Ultra-High Temperature
Materials (UHTMs) platform
provides open online access to
experimental data published in
scientific papers on these
remarkable materials. Our
materials explorer, equipped
with powerful and diverse
search filters and cutting-edge
programming technology,
empowers users to view, review,
and analyze a wide range of
properties.
